⚡ What Does Connecting Strava Mean?

When you connect your Strava account to our running quest platform, you're essentially:

  • 🗺️ Syncing Your Adventures: Your runs, hikes, and activities automatically appear in your quest log
  • 🏆 Unlocking Peak Conquests: We detect when you summit peaks and mountains, adding them to your collection
  • 📈 Tracking Your Progress: Your distance, elevation, and achievements contribute to group quests and personal goals
  • 🎯 Joining Group Quests: Collaborate with other adventurers on shared goals and challenges
  • 📊 Viewing Real-time Stats: See your year-to-date progress, peak summits, and activity history

🔒 Important: We only access your public activity data and never modify or delete anything on your Strava account.

🛡️ How We Protect Your Quest Data

Your data security is our top priority. Here's how we handle your information:

📦 What We Store:

  • Your Strava athlete ID (public identifier)
  • Activity data: runs, hikes, distance, elevation, dates
  • Peak summit records and achievements
  • Group membership and goal progress
  • Access tokens (encrypted) for syncing data

🔐 How We Protect It:

  • All data is encrypted and stored securely
  • We never share your personal information with third parties
  • Access tokens are refreshed automatically and stored safely
  • Your data is only used to enhance your quest experience

⏱️ Data Retention:

  • Your data remains active as long as your account is connected
  • Inactive accounts (no Strava connection) may be cleaned up after extended periods
  • You can request complete data deletion at any time using the tool below

🗑️ Request Account Deletion

Need to completely remove your data from our quest platform? No worries, adventurer! You can request a full account deletion below.

⚠️ Important Information:

  • Complete Data Wipe: This will permanently delete all your activities, peak summits, and group memberships
  • Group Quest Impact: You'll be removed from all group quests immediately
  • Reversible Process: You can always reconnect your Strava later and start fresh
  • Progress Recovery: While your historical data will be gone, your actual Strava activities remain untouched

🔑 Find Your Strava Athlete ID

Your Strava Athlete ID is the number in your Strava profile URL. For example, if your profile is https://strava.com/athletes/12345678, your ID is 12345678.
